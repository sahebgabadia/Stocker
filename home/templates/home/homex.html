About Stocker


Stocker is an online platform which allows you to shop electronic products, and what makes it different is that the price keeps changing. We're not talking about discounts, our website gives you the opportunity to buy your desired products at a lower price! And this price is based purely on your luck.
We have 5 companies associated with us, Samsung, Apple, One Plus, Bose and HP. Each of these companies provides a variety of their products for you to choose from. Once you've chosen a product, you can compare it's original price with the current price and if it fits well, then grab it!

Why Stocker


Stocker give you an opportunity to save money on a variety of products without compromising quality. And the catch is that there's no catch. The price of the product that you're interested in, depends solely on the stock of the company that product belongs to. There's an algorithm that runs within Stocker, which produces a price as a by product of the stock of the company. So, the price is always changing, it may increase or decrease within minutes. Stocker keeps it interesting, if you think the current price is the best bet then you better get it before it's too late!
